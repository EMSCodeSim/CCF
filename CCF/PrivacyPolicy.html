Privacy Policy

CCF CPR Timer

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

CCF CPR Timer (“the App”) is designed for CPR training and timing. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your choices.

Information We Collect

1) Information You Provide

The App does not require you to create an account or provide personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

2) Automatically Collected Information

The App may collect non-personal, anonymous information to help improve performance and functionality, including:

  • App usage data (e.g., session timing results)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)
  • Crash or diagnostic data

This information does not identify you personally.

3) Local Storage

Training session data (such as CPR time, pause duration, and calculated CCF results) is stored locally on your device only.

  • It is not transmitted to our servers
  • It is not shared with instructors, organizations, or third parties
  • It can be cleared by uninstalling the App or clearing app data

Advertising

The App may display advertisements only on the Reports page.

Ads may be provided by third-party advertising partners (such as Google AdMob), who may collect anonymous data such as:

  • Device identifiers
  • General location (non-precise)
  • Ad interaction data

This data is used to show relevant ads and measure ad performance.

Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

How We Use Information

Information collected by the App is used solely to:

  • Provide CPR timing and reporting functionality
  • Improve app performance and reliability
  • Display advertisements on the Reports page

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties.

Anonymous analytics or advertising data may be shared with service providers strictly for app operation and ad delivery.

Children’s Privacy

The App is intended for training and educational use. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us so it can be removed.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You may stop all data collection by uninstalling the App.
  • You may limit ad personalization through your device’s advertising settings.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new “Last updated” date.
